Common questions

Is my baby's audio recorded?
No. Cry detection runs entirely on your device. Audio is analyzed in memory and immediately discarded — it is never recorded, stored, or uploaded.

Where are sleep logs kept?
On your device only. They are never uploaded.

Is Sleepybee a medical device?
No. Sleepybee is a sleep aid and monitoring aid — not a medical device. It cannot detect breathing problems or prevent SIDS, and it is not a substitute for adult supervision. Always follow safe-sleep guidance.
