11 sounds that actually help

White noise, shushing, heartbeat, womb sounds, a music-box lullaby, rain, ocean and more — every one generated live for perfectly smooth, all-night play. Tap any sound to see the science behind it.

👂

Hears what you might miss

Night Watch listens for crying using on-device intelligence. Audio is analyzed on your phone and instantly discarded — never recorded, never uploaded.

🔔

Alerts on every device

One phone stays in the nursery. Yours — or your partner's — gets woken the moment crying is detected. Anywhere, even with the app closed.

🌙

Built for the whole night

Gentle fade-outs, dim sensory displays, a bedside Nightstand mode, and Auto-Soothe that softly raises the sound to help baby resettle.

📊

Know how every night went

Automatic sleep logs: cries, quiet stretches, full night timelines, and weekly charts against age-based recommendations.

🛡️

Safety first

Volume capped for infant hearing safety, distance guidance built in, and honest evidence labels on every sound.

Private by design

Your baby's audio is never recorded or uploaded. Sleep logs never leave your phone. The only things our servers ever see are your email and the ping that wakes your other devices. Read the full privacy policy →
